Key Strategic Insights
  • Retargeting — Subscribe (Meta) was the absolute strongest performer: ₹35,000 spend drove 399 conversions at an outstanding 6.83x ROAS and the lowest CPA (₹87.72) across all campaigns. This confirms that warm-audience retargeting converts far more efficiently than cold reach.
  • Performance Max — Gifting (Google) delivered the second-best ROAS (6.43x) on the back of a higher ₹1,299 gift-box Average Order Value (AOV), despite fewer total conversions. This makes it an ideal candidate for aggressive scaling ahead of upcoming festive events.
  • Brand Awareness — Reels and Search — Competitor Terms were the bottom performance drivers (2.50x and 2.44x ROAS respectively). However, since both fulfill top-of-funnel discovery goals rather than direct immediate purchase, they should be benchmarked on reach and consideration metrics rather than pure ROAS.
  • Meta out-performed Google slightly on blended ROAS (4.95x vs 4.79x) and significantly on CPA (₹120.97 vs ₹164.84), primarily driven by the stellar retargeting numbers. On Google, the High-Intent Branded + Generic search campaign stood solid at 4.89x ROAS.
  • Week-over-Week Momentum: Overall efficiency scaled continuously throughout the month as creative and targeting optimized. Both CTR and conversion rate trended strongly upwards towards Weeks 4 and 5, boosted significantly by the organic integration of the #MyKaapiMoment giveaway and mid-summer special offers.

Strategic Action Items for August
  • Budget Optimization: Reallocate approximately ₹10,000/month away from the low-performing Search — Competitor Terms campaign and push it into Retargeting — Subscribe to lock in predictable, high-margin subscription loops from engaged audiences.
  • Scale Gift Offerings: Aggressively expand Performance Max — Gifting allocations ahead of the early August Independence Day campaigns. Gifting AOV nearly doubles the base standard subscription value, presenting an excellent opportunity to expand revenue volume.
  • Adjust Awareness Benchmarks: Maintain funding levels for Brand Awareness — Reels but formally decouple it from primary ROAS targets. Evaluate its success strictly on absolute Video Completion Rates (VCR) and Cost Per Thousand Impressions (CPM), utilizing its pixel data pool to feed retargeting funnels.
  • Synchronized Organic Boosts: Time high-performing user-generated creative pushes (such as the #MyKaapiMoment initiative) to directly overlay with organic content schedules. Historical data demonstrates that engagement peaks and Conversion Rates (CVR) are fully optimized during these unified intervals.
